CLINICBOOK OFFICIAL POLICIES & FAQ

1. REFUNDS & CANCELLATIONS
- Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the appointment are fully refundable.
- Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% cancellation fee.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

2. INSURANCE
- We currently don't accept any INSURANCE

3. EMERGENCY
- ClinicBook is for non-emergency consultations only.
- In case of a medical emergency, please call 911 or visit the nearest ER immediately.

4. PRESCRIPTIONS
- Doctors can issue digital prescriptions which are sent directly to your registered pharmacy.
- Narcotic pain medications cannot be prescribed via tele-health.

5. CLINIC HOURS
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed
